Travelouge: Puerto Rico

Overview: What is Puerto Rico?

Puerto rico is an archipelago in the Caribbean Sea consisting of the main island, four small islands, and hundreds of cays and islets. The island itself is small yet packs a ton of diverse scenery, sights, and culture. One of the island’s most famous wildlife residents is the coquí (koh-KEE), a dime-size frog thats name comes from the sound it makes.

Must See Spots!

  1. Wander the historic streets of Old San Juan!
  2. Vist El Yunque National Rain Forest
  3. Swim with the sea turtles at Flamenco Beach
  4. Discover some history over at San Juan National Historic Site
  5. Watch the world’s brightest bioluminescent bay glow as you paddle a glass-bottomed kayak at Bahai bioluminescente.

Friendly Visitor tips...

  • Learn some basic spanish phrases
  • Most public beaches dont have a lifegaurd on duty so swim at your own risk!
  • On that note make sure to pack for the beach and city and esecially for the warm weather!
  • BRING SUNSCREEN IT IS REALLY HOT IN PUERTO RICO
